Coquito

About the recipe

Coquito is a traditional Puerto Rican holiday drink, a rich, creamy, coconut-based cocktail often called "Puerto Rican eggnog" (though most authentic recipes are eggless). This simple blender recipe is a great starting point, with variations allowing for customization.

  • evaporated milk
  • sweetened condensed milk
  • coconut cream
  • coconut milk
  • vanilla extract
  • ground cinnamon and nutmeg
  1. Combine all liquid ingredients and spices in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth and fully combined
  3. Taste and adjust spices or rum as needed
  4. Pour into some container and chill for 4 hours or ideally overnight for best flavor and consistency
  5. Shake well before serving cold in small glassesw, garnished with cinnamon
