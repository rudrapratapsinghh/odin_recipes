Lasagna

About the recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish featuring wide, flat pasta sheets layered with rich fillings like tomato sauce (ragù), béchamel, cheeses (mozzarella, ricotta, Parmesan), and meats or vegetables, then baked until bubbly and golden, resulting in a comforting, savory meal with distinct layers

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • a meat sauce
  • a creamy cheese filling

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake and Rest
